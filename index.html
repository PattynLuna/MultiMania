Explore the VR Experience

Explore the world of Virtual Reality: the boundry between fantasy and reality has never been this thin because of Virtual Reality. You and your friends can now test how it is to see the world in a different view. Have fun exploring innovative games, heartwarming stories or brand new worlds. Together with your friends you'll be acquainted with different virtual reality systems.

You can choose from a wide range of games that you can play both individually and in groups: for example, you put on your VR glasses and you end up in a virtual play zone where you try to score as many points as possible with a laser sword. Or give the correct instructions to the person with the VR glasses and try to dismantle the bomb as a team before it explodes.
